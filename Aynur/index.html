About the game

This game is designed to help young graduates make informed decisions about their majors by providing a fun and engaging platform for exploration. With this game, you can explore a variety of majors and careers, learning about the skills and knowledge required for each path and discovering what best fits your interests and aspirations. We believe that choosing a major should be an exciting and empowering experience, and that's exactly what our game aims to provide. This is more than just a tool for choosing a major - it's a fun and interactive way to learn about yourself and your potential. Whether you're undecided or just looking to explore your options further, our game is the perfect tool to help you discover your passion and take the next step towards a fulfilling career.

How to play?

Playing our game is easy and intuitive. To start, simply create an account and select your preferred avatar. Then, you'll be taken on a journey of exploration where you'll be presented with various majors and career paths to choose from. Each path comes with its own set of challenges and tasks, which will test your knowledge and skills. As you progress, you'll earn points and rewards that can be used to unlock new levels and paths. Our game is designed to be fun and engaging, with colorful graphics and intuitive gameplay that will keep you entertained for hours. So what are you waiting for? Join the adventure and discover your passion today!

Earn coins

Coins are an important part of our game and can be earned by answering questions correctly. Each time you successfully answer a question, you'll earn a certain number of coins that can be used to unlock new levels and features. In particular, coins can be used to charge up the bus, which allows you to travel freely between the different departments in the city. So be sure to answer as many questions as you can to earn coins and unlock all the exciting features our game has to offer!
